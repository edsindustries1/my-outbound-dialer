CRM & Data Sync

Works With The Tools You Already Use

Every completed call is automatically logged to your CRM or spreadsheet. No manual entry, no tab-switching, no data loss — it just happens in the background.

HubSpot

Paste your Private App token and every call is automatically logged as a HubSpot activity. Contacts are created or updated the moment a call ends — no CRM data entry required.

GoHighLevel

Connect your GHL Location API Key and sync call outcomes directly into your pipeline. Hot lead transfers, voicemail drops, call duration — all written automatically to the right contact.

Pipedrive

Log calls against existing Pipedrive deals and contacts using your API token. Every dial outcome — answered, voicemail, transferred — is recorded the instant the call completes.

Google Sheets

Write every call to a Google Sheet in real time. Nine columns auto-populated: Timestamp, Phone, From, Status, Transferred, Voicemail Dropped, AMD Result, Duration, and Hangup Cause.

Outbound Webhooks

Fire a signed POST request to any URL the moment a call ends. Works with Zapier, Make, n8n, or any custom endpoint — pipe your call data into any workflow automation tool.
