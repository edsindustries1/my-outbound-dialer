No corporate story. No "our team of 50 experts." Just one founder solving one problem — and being honest about it.

Who Built This

Hi, I'm Alex.

I built Open Humana because I watched small sales teams waste half their day on calls that go nowhere — listening to rings, leaving voicemails, logging dead ends. The closers were good. The process was broken. I figured AI could fix the boring part so the humans could focus on the human part. So I built it. No VC money. No big team. Just me, working on this every single day because I genuinely believe it solves a real problem that costs real businesses real money.

Where We Are

We're early. Here's what that means for you.

Open Humana is not a 50-person company with a $10 million runway. It's a focused product built for one purpose — giving small and mid-size sales teams the outbound firepower that used to cost $5,000 a month in human labor. We're onboarding our first customers now and I'm obsessive about making sure the product works exactly as described before we scale. That means you get something most software companies can't offer — direct access to the person who built it.
