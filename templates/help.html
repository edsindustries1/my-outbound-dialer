Open Humana is a digital employee agency that provides AI-powered sales agents for your business. Our flagship digital employee, Alex, handles outbound calls, drops personalized voicemails, transfers live calls to your team, and works 24/7 without breaks. Think of it as hiring a tireless teammate with superpowers who never calls in sick — all for a fraction of the cost of a human BDR.
When Alex detects a voicemail box, it automatically drops a pre-recorded or AI-generated personalized voicemail. Each message can include the prospect's name, company, property address, and other custom details — making it sound like a genuine, personal call. You can choose from standard templates or create fully personalized voicemails using AI voice cloning that sounds exactly like you.
Alex speaks 50+ languages for voicemail delivery and live interactions, with natural intonation and pronunciation tailored to your prospect's language preference. From English and Spanish to Mandarin, Arabic, and beyond — Alex adapts seamlessly to your global audience.
